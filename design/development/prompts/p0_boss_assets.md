## Shared Direction

Call Tyrant source art is the hard reference for body shape, silhouette, device
shell, warning language, and red/cyan palette. Do not replace it with a generic
phone monster. Generated assets may improve readability for gameplay scale, but
must remain visibly derived from the source concept and animation.

## Call Tyrant Boss Concept

Create a hand-painted dream boss concept based on the Call Tyrant hard
references: corrupted ringing-phone nightmare, black device-shell body, red
notification light, cyan waveform limbs, vibration pulse aura, readable
top-down / angled gameplay silhouette, able to summon black mud adds and throw
ringing phone fragments. Not photorealistic, no brand logos, no gore.

## Call Tyrant Warning Effects

Transparent gameplay VFX concepts derived from the source animation language:
circular summon ring with waveform marks, red-cyan throw arc warning, vibrating
notification pulse, painterly dream style, readable over dark bedroom floor.

## Boss Route Node Icon

Small route-map boss node icon derived from the Call Tyrant source silhouette:
red notification eye inside black phone crescent, cat-dream UI style, readable
at 64x64 and 128x128. Do not use text or phone-brand details.
