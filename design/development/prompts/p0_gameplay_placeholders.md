# P0 Gameplay Placeholder Prompts

Status: draft only. Use after style anchors are approved.

## Shared Direction

Create transparent-background gameplay placeholder sprites that match the P0
style anchors. Prefer readable silhouette, simple lighting, and in-game scale
clarity over illustration detail.

For Saiban, Nephthys, and Suzune, the colored turnaround files are mandatory
hard references. Preserve their exact coat colors, eye colors, clothing, weapons,
props, silhouettes, and compact upright proportions. Do not reinterpret them as
generic quadruped cats and do not use the generated starter lineup to override
the turnaround.

For enemies and bedroom interaction props, the design-source concept, animation,
and sprite sheets listed below are mandatory hard references. Generated style
anchors may guide mood, lighting, and rendering finish only; they must not
override source silhouettes, palettes, object identity, or gameplay readability.
